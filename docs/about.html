Hey! I am Daljeet Singh Lotey (@djtsingh). I build software that matters: from scalable systems, to creative tools and experiments (cool projects) in my free time. I'm a recent CS grad with a background in data, algorithms, and full-stack engineering.

I’m a total builder at heart, I love picking up new tech and seeing what I can create with it. That curiosity is what drove me to build Janus to stop bots, dive deep into computer vision for my research paper, and why I'm currently obsessing over data patterns for my market prediction project. I just really enjoy the process of learning, breaking things, and making them work better.

I’m also big on backing up that experimentation with solid foundations. You can check out my certifications to see the formal skills behind the code. My interests include machine learning, product design, and open-source tooling. When not coding, I like to explore computational design and gaming.
